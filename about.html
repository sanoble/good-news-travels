About Us

Good News Travels is a motorcoach travel company located out of Sullivan, IN. We provide group coach travel service custom designed to meet the needs of churches, school corporations, clubs, and other organizations. We provide trip scheduling, meal accommodations, luggage handling service, step on guides, hotel arrangements, and provide a trip host to serve your every need.

Are you a member of a group or organization that would like to travel? Are you in a leadership position and desire to promote a group trip, but do not have time to plan or promote such a project? Good News Travels is the answer!

We are a tour service providing complete travel arrangements for groups from single day events to multi-day events.

We specialize in providing custom all-inclusive tour packages made to order for your group.

Good News Travels was founded in 1994 by Dennis and Sharon Piety. Truly loving to travel our beautiful country, Dennis and Sharon introduced Good News Travels to allow others affordable travel to wonderful destinations. Deciding to slow down into semi-retirement, the Piety's sold Good News Travels to Troy Orwig in 2003, while still helping out on certain trips as driver and hostess.
